About Re-Fresh

We're on a mission to revolutionize the energy drink industry with clean, sustainable energy that actually works.

> Our Story

Re-Fresh was born from a simple frustration: why do energy drinks have to be loaded with sugar, artificial ingredients, and leave you crashing by 3PM? We set out to create something different—a clean energy drink that powers your day without the compromise.

Founded in Huddersfield, UK, we're a team of health-conscious creators, athletes, and professionals who believe energy should enhance your performance, not derail it. Every can of Re-Fresh is crafted with precision, using only the highest quality ingredients to deliver sustained focus and energy.

> Our Mission

To provide clean, sustainable energy that supports your lifestyle without the sugar spikes, artificial additives, or energy crashes. We're committed to transparency, quality, and helping you perform at your best—whether that's in the office, gym, or wherever your day takes you.

> What Makes Us Different

  • Zero Sugar: No hidden sugars or artificial sweeteners—just clean, natural flavor.
  • Clean Caffeine: 160mg from green coffee beans for smooth, sustained energy without jitters.
  • Electrolytes: Stay hydrated while you power through your day.
  • B-Vitamins: Support mental clarity and focus for peak performance.
  • Vegan & Gluten-Free: Made for everyone, regardless of dietary preferences.

> Our Values

At Re-Fresh, we believe in doing things the right way. That means quality ingredients, transparent labeling, sustainable practices, and a commitment to your health and performance. We're not just another energy drink—we're your partner in achieving your goals.
