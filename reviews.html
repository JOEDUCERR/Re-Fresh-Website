FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Is Re-Fresh sugar free?

Yes. Re-Fresh has zero sugar and only 10 calories per can. We use a light, modern sweetness for a clean taste.

How much caffeine is in a can?

Each can has 160mg of caffeine — about the same as a strong cup of coffee, balanced with L-theanine for smooth focus.

Can I drink more than one can a day?

We recommend starting with one can and not exceeding two per day. Everyone’s caffeine tolerance is different — listen to your body.
